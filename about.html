Projects About Resume

I design products by making sense of complexity.

I’m a product and UX designer with over ten years of experience. My background in advertising and visual design really shaped how I work today. It taught me that while the details matter, I’m much more interested in how the whole system works and how products grow over time.

I enjoy stepping in when things aren't fully defined yet. I like taking messy requirements or complex ideas and turning them into clear flows and structures that make sense for the user and the business.

For me, design is a team sport. I love working closely with product and engineering to figure out the best solutions together. I try to think about the long-term impact of our decisions, making sure we’re building something scalable and not just a quick fix.

I’m naturally drawn to products that solve real needs, like fintech or tools that people rely on every day. I really value honest communication and working with teams that prioritize clarity and quality.
